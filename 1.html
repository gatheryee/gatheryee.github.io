About this blog

Welcome! This is the first post of this blog and it intends to serve the purpose of introducing and explaining the purpose of this brand new website.

A little about myself

Hello, my name is Louis and I am from Hong Kong. I am currently a university student majoring in Law and I love playing Magic the Gathering in my free time. In terms of Magic-related accomplishments, I really haven’t got any. I am probably one of the better players are my LGS where I 3-0 FNM drafts at a fairly regular rate. But on the competitive level, I haven’t even reach day 2 of a GP yet. My closest to ever doing so was when I finished 5-4 in GP Beijing 2017 and GP Kyoto 2017. On both occasions, I lost the “win-and-in” round 9. So my biggest accomplishment so far is only a top 8 finish in a PTQ.

The reason for starting this blog

The reason for starting this blog is simple. I found that I consume a lot of Magic-related content on a daily basis and I started to feel that there isn’t enough of such content to satisfy my desire. The solution I came up with is to become a content creator myself. In fact, I have always had many views and opinions on Magic-related matters which I wanted to share and this blog can now serve as a platform for me to do so.

The aim of this blog

As mentioned above, I am not a very competitive player in terms of skill and achievements to date. As a result, I do not consider myself qualified to write posts on strategy and tactics. Instead, this blog will focus on discussing and examining controversial or otherwise interesting Magic-related issues/news.
